A Valuable Aid To Learning MATLAB(R)

MATLAB Primer, Fourth Edition

Author - Kermit Sigmon

Department of Mathematics, University of Florida, Gainesville

DESCRIPTION

This Fourth Edition reflects an extensive general revision of the MAT LAB Primer into a more compact, reader-friendly bound format. However, its hands-on approach retains the original purpose of the earlier editions-to help s tudents and others begin to use MATLAB.

FEATURES

  • Based on MATLAB Versions 4.0, 4.1, and 4.2
  • Features of Version 4.2 blended into the text
  • Substantial new section on the Symbolic Math Toolbox, which merges the symbo lic capabilities of the software Maple(R) with the numerical capabilities of MAT LAB
  • Extensive new section on graphics
  • Fine-grained Contents and detailed Index
