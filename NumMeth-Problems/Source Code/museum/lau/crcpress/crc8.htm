This book presents new original numerical methods that have been developed to the stage of concrete algorithms and successfully applied to practical problems in mathematical physics. The book discusses new methods for solving stiff syste ms of ordinary differential equations, stiff elliptic problems encountered in pr oblems of composite material mechanics, Navier-Stokes systems, and nonstationary problems with discontinuous data. These methods allow natural paralleling of al gorithms and will find many applications in vector and parallel computers.
